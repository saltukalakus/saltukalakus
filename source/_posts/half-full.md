Artificial intelligence (AI) has wide range of potential applications. I can simply group the possible applications in two big clusters. First is the vision based applications. The other cluster is the speech and text processing applications.

Having intelligence in vision has great number of possible use case in industrial products. 
It can produce a lot of value for  manufacturing and product testing. 
AI in vision have great use cases like security. 

Having intelligence in speech and text processing has a lot of possible use case in artificial personal assistants, chat bots, big data analytics. AI in text processing can detect fraud, analyze social media etc.
